AZALEA ROADMAP 2026

Our vision for Azalea is to create the most atmospheric and dynamic survival experience in DayZ. Here is what we are working on next:

[ Q3 2026 - The Industrial Expansion ]

We are focusing heavily on the Northern territories. Expect a massive new industrial zone complete with functional train yards, deep-shaft mines, and high-risk environmental hazards.

- Expansion of the Northern Industrial Zone

- Dynamic weather events (Acid Rain, Ash Storms)

- Custom UI elements for player stats and progression

- Complete overhaul of the vehicle repair mechanics

[ Q4 2026 - The Winter Freeze ]

As the year ends, Azalea will be covered in snow. Survival will require managing body temperature, finding shelter, and hunting scarce wildlife.

- Full winter season integration with dynamic snow accumulation

- New Tier 4 military installations hidden in the ice

- Advanced player grouping and territory UI

- Bear and wolf pack AI overhaul

*Note: The roadmap is subject to change based on community feedback and BetaHub priority reports.*
