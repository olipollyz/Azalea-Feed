v0.40 — The Eruption Update

[MAJOR FEATURES]

+ The Volcanic Eruption: Mount Azalea now periodically erupts. Stay clear of the ash clouds and watch out for dynamic lava flows!

+ The Underworld: A sprawling new network of caves beneath the volcano. Requires a gas mask and light source.

+ Completely overhauled the Southern Coastline with new fishing villages and dynamic boat spawns.

[BALANCE & TWEAKS]

+ Rebalanced Tier 3 and 4 loot tables. High-end military gear is now significantly rarer outside of the volcano zone.

+ Increased the spawn rate of NBC gear in medical facilities.

+ Reduced the aggregate ambient noise volume during extreme weather events.

[BUG FIXES]

- Fixed a client crash caused by C.O.T admin teleports during camera shake events.

- Resolved an issue where vanilla dynamic music would unintentionally play over the main menu theme.

- Repaired broken navmesh bounds in the Northern Industrial Park.

- Fixed a null pointer crash on respawn related to the login time base script.

Thank you for participating in the Azalea Beta! Be sure to report any bugs to the BetaHub.
